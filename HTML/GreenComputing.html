How to dispose of your old computer and/or hard driveâ€¦
The first thing you want to do is backup your data to another source. Ensure all old photos, games, music, and other documents have been saved to an external source. After ensuring the data has been saved elsewhere, the next thing to do is wipe the hard drive. Delete all files and perform an operating system hard-reset. Make sure to leave the computer and hard drive empty of all sensitive or personal information. Donate or resell: you may be able to pass the computing device to friends or family, however there are organizations that supply working laptops and computers to at-risk students and families. Whatever you choose to do with your old computing devices, do not dispose of them in the conventional trash as they contain hazardous chemicals. Should you remove your hard drive before recycling?

While erasing a hard drive before recycling will help prevent your data from being stolen, going a step further and destroying the data will GUARANTEE that it is safe.

    You can destroy a hard drive a number of creative ways:
  • drilling through the hard drive
  • smash it will a hammer
  • set it on fire
  • magnetizing the hard drive
